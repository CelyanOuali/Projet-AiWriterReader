COLD FISH?

Miles and miles into his life, the man still didn't know the answer to why he was this long dead. If he'd tried to take off the body, his brain wouldn't have worked. He'd already tried. And he couldn't get an answer.

It wasn't just that he had no idea his partner had turned him on. He'd tried to make it impossible.

"Do we really need to know?" asked a voice at the mouth. I saw the man shift uncomfortably, but when I looked up, he was already in front of me.

"Of course not. We're all alone with two people dead." He paused, then said something that was a little less self-assured.
